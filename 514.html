Professor Utonium's accidental Powerpuff Girl recipe

Professor Utonium was a scientific genius who dedicated his life to his craft. So much so, he never started a family or made any friends. In his lab one day, he wanted to create the perfect daughters to solve his lonliness problem. While adding his ingredients, he accidentally added a chemical component which altered his recipe and in turn gave his lab created daughters super hero powers!

This is how you create the Powerpuff girls!

These are the ingredients you need:

  • Sugar
  • Spice
  • Everything nice
  • Chemical X

These are the steps you need to take to make this recipe:

  1. In a large pot, add the sugar.
  2. Add the spice.
  3. Add everything nice and stir.
  4. Add chemical X.

FOR DRAMATIC EFFECT, WHILE YOU ARE STIRING IN THE INGREDIENTS IN STEP 3, ACCIDENTALLY KNOCK IN THE INGREDIENTS IN STEP 4 LIKE PROFESSOR UTONIUM DID.
